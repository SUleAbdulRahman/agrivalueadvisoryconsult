A note on USAID

USAID Feed the Future funding to Ghana was suspended in January 2025 and has not been restored. Our Funding-Ready Pack now focuses on the funders that remain genuinely active: IFAD/MOFA, AfDB, and GIZ. Read our full USAID status update for the details.

What is the Funding-Ready Pack?

Preparing a competitive grant application takes more than filling out a form. It requires a clear diagnosis of the problem you are solving, a credible financing or leverage plan, confirmed partnerships, and documentation that most organisations do not have assembled by default. The Funding-Ready Pack is our end-to-end service for getting your organisation from "we need funding" to "submission-ready."

Who this is for

Agribusinesses, farmer-based organisations, cooperatives, and NGOs in Ghana seeking grant or co-financing support from development finance institutions, and who need help identifying the right funder and building a genuinely competitive application.

Funders we work with

IFAD / MOFA GASIPAfDB Agriculture Fast TrackGIZ Agribusiness Facility for Africa

IFAD / MOFA — GASIP Matching Grants

Sub-grants for farmer-based organisations, cooperatives, and agribusiness SMEs, with a strong focus on smallholder value chain development in Northern Ghana.

AfDB — Agriculture Fast Track (AFT) Fund

Grants of $50,000–$1.5 million to prepare larger agribusiness investments — feasibility studies, environmental assessments, and financial modelling — for projects valued at $1 million or more.

GIZ — Agribusiness Facility for Africa (ABF)

Matching grants for private sector-led agribusiness projects across ECOWAS countries, with particular focus on cashew, maize, and livestock value chains.

How it works

  1. 01

  2. 02
    Funder matchingBased on your value chain, geography, and organisational stage, we identify the strongest-fit funder for your application.

  4. 04

  5. 05
    Submission coordinationWe coordinate the final package and support you through the submission process itself.
