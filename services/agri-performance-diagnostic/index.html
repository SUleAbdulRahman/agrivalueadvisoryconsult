Agri-Performance Diagnostic™

A structured operational health check that identifies inefficiencies, bottlenecks, and performance gaps in your agribusiness — delivered with a clear, prioritised improvement roadmap.

PRO: 7–10 working daysPREMIUM: 14–21 working days

What is the Agri-Performance Diagnostic?

The Agri-Performance Diagnostic is a structured assessment of your agribusiness's operations, finances, and market position. We look across 10 dimensions of your business — from workflow and financial management to post-harvest handling and market access — and give you an evidence-based picture of where your business is strong, where it is leaking value, and what to fix first.

This is not a generic audit template. Every diagnostic is built around field visits, stakeholder interviews, and document review specific to your operation — whether you are a grain aggregator in Tamale, a shea processor in Northern Ghana, or a poultry business in Accra.

Who this is for

Agro-processors, grain aggregators, agri-startups, cooperatives, and SME food businesses who suspect something in their operation is underperforming but need an outside, evidence-based view to know exactly what and why.

What the diagnostic covers

Operational workflowFinancial managementHuman resources Input supply chainProduction systemsPost-harvest handling Market accessTechnology useCompliance & governance Strategic clarity

How it works

  1. 01
    Discovery callA 30-minute conversation to understand your business, your concerns, and what a successful outcome looks like for you.
  2. 02
    Document reviewWe review your financial records, production data, and existing processes against a structured checklist.
  3. 03
    Field visit & interviewsWe visit your operation and speak with key staff to see how things actually run day to day.
  4. 04
    Scoring & analysisWe score your business across all 10 dimensions and identify specific points of profit leakage.
  5. 05
    Report & roadmapYou receive a full diagnostic report and a prioritised, practical improvement roadmap — then we walk through it together in a debrief session.

"The goal is not a report that sits in a drawer. It is a clear, prioritised list of what to fix first — and why it matters to your bottom line."

What you receive

  • Operational Assessment Summary — a clear picture of how your business is performing across all 10 dimensions
  • Workflow Review Findings — specific bottlenecks and inefficiencies identified during field visits and interviews
  • Key Inefficiency Analysis — where value is being lost, and by how much where quantifiable
  • Recommendations Report — practical, prioritised actions specific to your business
  • Improvement Roadmap — a sequenced plan for implementing the recommendations
  • Debrief Session — a walkthrough of the findings with your team, not just a document handed over
