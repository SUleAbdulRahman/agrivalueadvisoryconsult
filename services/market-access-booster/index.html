Market Access Booster™

Market linkage and commercial buyer connection support — we research your value chain, identify qualified buyers, facilitate introductions, and support commercial agreement development across Ghana and West Africa.

PRO: 4–6 weeksPREMIUM: 8–12 weeks

What is the Market Access Booster?

Many agribusinesses in Ghana produce good quality output but struggle to find reliable, well-paying buyers. The Market Access Booster closes that gap. We map your value chain, identify qualified commercial buyers — aggregators, processors, exporters, and institutional buyers — and facilitate real introductions and commercial agreements, not just a list of contacts.

Who this is for

Agribusinesses, cooperatives, and agro-processors in Ghana who have production capacity and quality product, but need structured support finding and closing deals with reliable buyers.

Buyer types we work with

Grain aggregatorsAgro-processorsInstitutional buyers Export buyersSupermarket chainsNGO & DFI off-takers

How it works

  1. 01
    Product profilingWe document your commodity specifications, production capacity, certifications, and quality standards.
  2. 02
    Value chain mappingWe map the full value chain for your commodity, identifying every relevant buyer type and market entry point.
  3. 03
    Buyer research & screeningWe build a longlist of 20–30 buyers and screen it down to a qualified shortlist matched to your specs and capacity.
  4. 04
    Outreach & introductionsWe reach out to priority buyers on your behalf and arrange buyer-seller meetings.
  5. 05
    Agreement supportWe support commercial agreement discussions and review draft off-take agreements before you sign.

"A buyer list is not market access. Market access is a signed agreement with a buyer who pays reliably and keeps buying next season."

What you receive

  • Market Assessment Report — value chain overview, price analysis, and buyer landscape for your commodity
  • Buyer Shortlist — 8–12 qualified buyers matched to your product and capacity
  • Facilitated Introductions — real meetings arranged with priority buyers, not just contact details
  • Agreement Review — support reviewing draft off-take or partnership agreements before signature
  • Linkage Summary Report — a record of buyers contacted, meetings held, and agreements in progress

Have product but need reliable buyers?

Tell us about your commodity and capacity — we'll give you an honest read on the market opportunity before you commit to anything.
